DIGITAL CASE

FOR BEAUTY BOMB

cosmetics for teens

 

TASK:

Beauty Bomb was a new cosmetic brand that is targeting the low budget segment. It's very cheap and bright — perfect for teens, but we had to convince them that they should turn their attention to Beauty Bomb, which was just launched in Russia.

SOLUTION:

Teenage girls watch video tutorials of the beauty bloggers and try to do fashionable makeup, but they are too shy to go out with it.

Therefore, in social networks, we have created a platform that unites all these girls and tells them that there are no rules in beauty, and it doesn’t matter what Mom or teachers at school will say about your appearance.


Girls perform various beauty-challenges with Beauty Bomb cosmetics, share their make up, check out diverse and bold results of the other participants, in order to overcome their fear of judgemental comments and become a part of a large beauty gang. 

 

MY ROLE:

Creating the concept of all digital communications, e.g.

- idea

- concept of visual style

- activations to launch brand & new collections

- development of UGC-activities

- video script writing

- pitching for client
- supervising content production

 


RESULTS:

- 2 weeks after the release of the brand, cosmetics were sold out throughout Russia
- Until now, the brand exists with the platform #BeautyWithoutRules and enjoys huge popularity throughout the country
