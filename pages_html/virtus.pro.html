SPECIAL PROJECT

FOR VIRTUS.PRO

russian e-sports project

 

TASK:

In 2018 Virtus.pro sets a historic achievement: 4 wins in major tournaments for the calendar year. We needed to create a special project about it with a support of the team sponsor Parimatch. The project should consist of 7 materials about the matches.

SOLUTION:

The main page of the special project is framed as a map for Dota 2. Runes containing interesting facts about the team are scattered across the map. Information about the sponsor is highlighted on the map as in-game shop.


The map itself is divided into 7 sections as in the game. Each section is linked to an article about one of the matches. Within a week after the launch, sections were unlocked one by one daily.


When an article is being read, the related map element (towers, Roshan or Throne) is destroyed. Once one's read all 7 articles, the winner’s cup appears on the map. It contains the information about Virtus.pro's upcoming games and a gift from the team’s sponsor.
