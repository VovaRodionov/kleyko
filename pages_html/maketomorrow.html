B2B COMMS PLATFORM

FOR TIKTOK FOR BUSINESS

Cyprus

 

TASK:

Create a concept of comms platform for highlighting the emerge of TikTok in Cyprus and support the first ever TikTok Cyprus Summit that helps local and global brands start their own business adventure with TikTok.

SOLUTION:

The comms platform "Make Tomorrow". This concept combines the ability to show the innovation of TikTok, which has already been proven by a number of projects. As well as show the future opportunities for brands who want to grow their business and determine what tomorrow will be like for millions of people around the planet.

The platform combined an educational event, a promotional campaign about new business opportunities, and personal partner meetings with brands.

MY ROLE:

Leading the in-house creative team, creating the concept of the event & promo campaign, supervising production of all comms materials, collaborating with cross-functional departments
